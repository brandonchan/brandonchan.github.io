Conflict

Week 8

Discuss a time you had a conflict. What was it about? What happened?

I feel like I always run into conflicts playing basketball. My last conflict must have been my teammate turning the ball over enough to lose the game. Although I take "Rec" leagues casually. I still want to win or lose on my own terms, not to give up the game due to lack of IQ or skills.

What was the source of the conflict?

Team mate thinks hes good, but he's not. And myself for not taking control of the game when we needed it. Also losing sucks.

What basic emotions did you feel upon analyzing the conflict?

I'm irate. Losing is one thing. Losing because your teammate is awful at baksetball is another. I am pretty livid that I exit the game early.

What actions did you take to resolve your conflict?

I tell it to his face that he's turning the ball over too much. I'm pretty stern and I tell everyone that we are losing because of it. Everyone is an adult so I explain it like an adult. No need to lose my mind over something so stupid.

If you could go back, what would you do differently, if anything?

I would never let this guy dribble or pass ever. He throws a bad pass, I need to get it. It doesn't work that way. I need to take charge and not let people who are unable to run point guard, handle the ball. I need to be the leader I can be. A floor general, no one likes to lose, but losing like this was hard.

What did you learn from this experience?

There is an opportunity to lead and an opportunity to sit back. I need to recognize that and lead when I'm able to because we are wasting our time allowing others to lose the game for us.
