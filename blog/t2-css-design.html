What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

Margin is like a buffer zone between two items like the edge of the screen and the content. Similar to finding a space on a wall for a picture. If it's in the middle of a giant wall. Your margins are huge. The border is like a frame of a picture. It keeps the content from being too close to the edges or other content. The padding is like the outside white space of a diploma. It centers content to be neat and inbetween the borders.

  • Margins = Space on the wall unused for your diploma
  • Border = Picture frame
  • Padding = White paper space on diploma that centers your diploma content.
